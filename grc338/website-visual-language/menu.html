Coffee

  • Americano

    A smooth and balanced espresso-based drink made by blending rich, freshly pulled espresso shots with hot water. The result is a bold yet approachable cup with the depth of espresso and the lightness of drip coffee. Perfect for anyone who enjoys a clean, straightforward coffee experience with a touch of sophistication.

    $2.00

  • Cappuccino

    A classic Italian favorite crafted with equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and velvety milk foam. This drink features a bold espresso base softened by a creamy texture, creating a harmonious balance between strength and sweetness. Ideal for those who appreciate a cozy, textured coffee.

    $3.55

  • Cold Brew

    Brewed slowly over many hours using chilled, filtered water to draw out smooth, naturally sweet flavors from the coffee grounds. This method produces a bold, low-acidity drink that is refreshing, crisp, and full of character. Served cold and often enjoyed black or with your choice of milk for a more indulgent finish.

    $5.00

  • Drip Coffee

    Our everyday staple, brewed fresh throughout the day for a clean, classic cup. Hot water passes steadily through freshly ground beans to create a bright, flavorful, and aromatic coffee. Simple by design and reliable in taste, drip coffee is the perfect go-to for a no-nonsense, satisfying brew.

    $1.50

  • Latte

    A creamy combination of rich espresso and silky steamed milk, topped with a light layer of foam. The milk mellows the intensity of the espresso, creating a smooth and comforting drink that pairs beautifully with flavored syrups or stands perfectly on its own. A versatile favorite for both new coffee drinkers and longtime enthusiasts.

    $4.25

  • Mocha

    A decadent blend of bold espresso, steamed milk, and chocolate syrup or cocoa, finished with a touch of foam or whipped cream. This drink offers the perfect harmony of coffee and rich chocolate, creating a smooth, dessert-like experience in every sip. Ideal for anyone who enjoys a sweet twist on their daily caffeine fix.

    $5.25
